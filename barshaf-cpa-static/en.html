About

Barshaf CPA is an accounting firm with two decades of expertise in accounting and business advisory services. The firm was founded by CPA Yaniv Barshaf and serves both large and small businesses, with local and international operations. CPA Barshaf held senior management positions at Somekh Chaikin (KPMG) and serves as a faculty member at the Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya and the College of Management Academic Studies, teaching financial and managerial accounting. CPA Barshaf holds a B.A. in Business Administration (Accounting) and an M.B.A. in Finance (with distinction), both from the College of Management Academic Studies. His extensive experience guarantees professional, uncompromising service while maintaining a direct and personal relationship with every client.
